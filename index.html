Why Choose Us

We Are Professional Logistics & Cargo Agency

RM Malizon Logistics Corp was established and registered as a corporation on July 29, 2022. We were formerly operating as RM Malizon Trucking Services which started in 2014. Over the years, we have taken pride in being able to offer our client the reasonable rates and excellent services. As a local family-owned business, we are always willing to do our best to make sure your experience with us is a pleasant one.

The company is a logistics company that specializes in BOC Accreditation, Permits & Licenses, Import and Export clearances, international freight forwarding and road transportation with main office in Batangas, Philippines. The business transports variety of general cargo intended for importation from its port of entry to its designated destination within the Philippines or for exportation to its designated port of exit.

Timeline

RM Malizon Logistics began by assisting companies with BOC accreditation, permits, and licenses, as well as handling their import and export clearances.
